What is PEDAL

Pedal is a space for showcasing amazingly unique bike builds. Where biker lovers of all kinds can come to admire other peoples builds and / or post an article about their own unique build. Whether its a refurbished 90's mtb a brand new carbon gravel bike or a vintage stell road racer, all is welcome. Nothing is too weird. Its also a great place to see how others have used specific bike components on their steeds.
